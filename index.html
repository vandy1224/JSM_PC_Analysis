Search methodology
Search Criteria
We conducted a literature search using PubMed for articles published from January 1, 2010, to January 1, 2025. Search terms included a combination of the MeSH terms “Pancreatic Neoplasms”, “carcinoma, pancreatic ductal”, “Early Detection of Cancer”, “Early Diagnosis”, “carcinoembryonic antigen”, and “Positron-Emission Tomography”. Inclusion Criteria
  • 1) Written in English
  • 2) subjects must be humans
  • 3) PC comparison analysis must specify PDAC cases
  • 4) tools are used for PC screening or early disease detection
  • 5) reports diagnostic metrics (sensitivity, specificity, AUC...
Exclusion Criteria
  • 1) review articles or prior meta-analyses
  • 2) included subjects in late-stage (III or IV) PDAC in all result analyses
  • 3) were animal or in vitro studies

Study Selection
Two independent reviewers screened titles and abstracts, then full-text review to assess eligibility.
Data Extraction
Data was extracted where possible for: Author, year, sample sizes, diagnostic modality, specific biomarker or tool, sensitivity (CI), specificity, AUC (CI), stage (prior to diagnosis or stages I or II)
