Background

The Pancreas

The pancreas is a organ that works for both the digestive and endocrine system, creating enzymes to assist in digestion and hormones to regulate blood sugar. The pancreas is located on the right side of your body behind parts of the duodenum, in front of your spine and is surrouneded by blood vessels, the gallbladder, liver and spleen. It is only about 6 inchese long and weighs about 91.8 grams

Pancreatic Cancer

Motivation

PC is often diagnosed at later stages, which greatly impacts prognosis. When screening for PC, it is difficult since PC has no strong biomarkers, local pancreatic tumors are relatively small (≤4cm), and it is a low lifetime risk cancer (around ~1%) [1, 2]. Additionally, during the early stages, patients are asymptomatic, with nonspecific symptoms appearing after metastasis [2, 3, 4]. At these later stages, the only curative therapy is surgical resection with supplementary chemotherapy, which improves prognosis[2, 3].

Meta-Analysis

We conducted a literature search using PubMed for articles published from January 1, 2010, to January 1, 2025. Search terms included a combination of the MeSH terms “Pancreatic Neoplasms”, “carcinoma, pancreatic ductal”, “Early Detection of Cancer”, “Early Diagnosis”, “carcinoembryonic antigen”, and “Positron-Emission Tomography”.

Inclusion Criteria

  • Studies must be written in English
  • Subjects must be humans
  • Studies must specify that the PC in analysis is PDAC
  • Studies report diagnostic metrics
  • The reported diagnostic metrics for tools are regarding PC screening or early disease detection

Exclusion Criteria

  • If the study is a review articles or meta-analyses
  • If the study included subjects in late-stage (III or IV) PDAC in all result analyses
  • If the study conducted animal or in vitro studies

Study Selection

Two independent reviewers screened titles and abstracts, then full-text review to assess eligibility.

Data Extraction

Data was extracted where possible for: Author, year, sample sizes, diagnostic modality, specific biomarker or tool, sensitivity (CI), specificity, AUC (CI), stage (prior to diagnosis or stages I or II)

Results

Blood Serum Results

We have identified 9 sources that meet our eligibility and test CA19-9 as an early diagnostic tool for PDAC.

Imaging Results

Currently only 5 sources that meet our eligibility and mention PET have been identified. We aim to continue gathering materials on imaging diagnostic tools and expanding to include CT, MRI and combinations of the three.
